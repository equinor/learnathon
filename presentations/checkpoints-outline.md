# Checkpoint Slides — Outline

Transition slides shown at each phase change throughout the day.

1. **Coffee Break** (9:30)
   - Back at 9:45

2. **Lab 1: Mini-spec + Plan** (9:45)
   - Write your mini-spec (use the template)
   - Ask your agent to plan
   - Review and push back or approve
   - Done when: spec committed, plan agreed, you know what to build first

3. **Lab 2: Build Sprint 1** (10:30)
   - Build your first working version
   - Commit often, even if rough
   - Ask for help if stuck

4. **Lunch** (11:30)
   - Commit what you have before you go
   - Back at 12:15

5. **Security Pulse** (12:15)
   - 5 minutes: run the security prompt on your codebase
   - Fix what you find
   - Found something interesting? That's a bingo square and a demo story

6. **Lab 3: Build Sprint 2** (12:30)
   - Polish, fix, prepare demo
   - 13:15: "45 minutes to demo time"
   - 13:30: "Stop features. Prepare what you'll say."

7. **Break — Prep for Demos** (13:45)
   - Check your bingo card — any easy squares left to fill?
   - Look at the **award categories** — which one is your best shot?
     - **Best Creation** — is your thing impressive/useful? Show it working.
     - **Most Creative AI Use** — did you use AI in an unexpected way? Highlight it.
     - **Best Safety Practice** — did you catch a security issue? Tell that story.
     - **Best Risk Catch** — did the AI get something wrong? That's gold.
     - **Best Fail Story** — did something go hilariously wrong? Own it.
     - **People's Choice** — what makes your team memorable?
   - You can aim for several, but focus is often the key to winning
   - What surprised you? What would you do differently?

8. **Show & Tell** (14:00)
   - 5 min per team: 2 min demo, 2 min learnings, 1 min Q&A

9. **Vote + Awards** (15:30)
   - Phones out, vote all 6 categories
   - Reveals one by one

10. **Done** (16:00)
    - Thank you!
