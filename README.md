# 🎓 The Learnathon

> **Learn by building, reflect by sharing, win by exploring**

A 4-hour hands-on learning event where teams explore AI-assisted software engineering tools and methods by building something real.

---

## Why?

- **Learn by doing** — not just watching presentations
- **Explore new tools** — Copilot, Claude Code, and more
- **Build community** — share experiences, learn from each other
- **Prepare for EDC** — test the format before the conference

---

## The Format

| Phase | Time | What Happens |
|-------|------|--------------|
| 🎯 **Welcome** | 15 min | Form teams of 2-3, get your Bingo card |
| 📚 **Learn** | 30 min | The Toolbox → Live Demo → Methods & Bingo → Tips & Gotchas |
| 🚀 **Challenge** | 15 min | Pick your mission, understand the rules |
| 🔨 **Build** | 1h 45m | Teams create, explore, experiment |
| ☕ **Break** | 15 min | Snacks, prep for demos |
| 🎤 **Demo** | 35 min | 7 teams × 5 min — show what you built + share learnings |
| 🗳️ **Vote** | 15 min | Audience votes on award categories |
| 🏆 **Awards** | 10 min | Winners announced, prizes, wrap-up |

**Total: 4 hours**

---

## 📚 Learn Phase Breakdown

| Part | Time | Content |
|------|------|---------||
| **The Toolbox** | 5 min | Quick overview: Copilot, Claude Code, MCP servers, agent mode |
| **Live Demo** | 10 min | Build something small, narrating your thinking and demonstrating methods |
| **Methods & Bingo** | 10 min | Walk through the Bingo card — explain each technique |
| **Tips & Gotchas** | 5 min | What AI gets wrong, how to spot risks, pro tips |

---

## Pick Your Challenge

Teams choose one mission:

| Challenge | What You Build |
|-----------|----------------|
| 🎮 **Conference Stand Game** | Fun interactive game for EDC booth |
| 🔌 **Useful MCP Server** | Reusable tool for the community |
| 🤖 **Custom Agent** | Shareable agent mode configuration |
| 💡 **BYOI (Bring Your Own Idea)** | Your passion project — must be demoable in 5 min |
